T/FSpan & independence

1. \(\mathbf{w}=(2,3,-2)\) is a linear combo of \(\mathbf{v}_1=(1,0,1)\) and \(\mathbf{v}_2=(0,1,0)\). T/F?

2. Span of \(n-1\) vectors of \(\mathbb{R}^n\) is a hyperplane. T/F?

Find all \(k\) such that \(6x+2y=0\), \(k^2x+y=0\), \(x-y+3z=0\) has only the trivial solution.
